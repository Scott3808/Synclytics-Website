The Problem

Fleet Systems Miss
What Trainers Need

Shift-Long Averages

Fleet management systems give you shift averages. Great for production, useless for training. You can't coach an operator on 12 hours of blended data.

No Stage Breakdown

You get total cycle time. But is the problem in the dig? The swing? The dump? Without stage-level data, you're guessing where the skill gap is.

No Training Context

Automated systems don't know you're running an assessment. They capture everything — including lunch breaks and shift changes.

Paper & Clipboard

So trainers go back to stopwatches and paper. Scratching down times that are hard to read, harder to compare, and impossible to trend over time.
